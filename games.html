Drone Annihilation: Blacksite

My first person shooter made in Unreal Engine entirely using Blueprints. The game can be played with mouse and keyboard but also has full gamepad support. There is a graphics settings menu with all graphics settings expected from a modern game from texture quality to anti aliasing. There is an optimal settings button which gets the users hardware and sets the graphics accordingly. The player can save these settings. The game includes a timer in which the player must destroy all drones on the map to win. The game has been set up as a more oldschool shooter. There are ammunition, health and time pickups. I you get too close to a drone, they will navigate towards you before exploding. If a drone is shot but not destroyed, they will aggro and navigate towards the player.

An Uafáis - The Horror

An Uafáis - The Horror was my final project for Game Development at ATU Sligo. Made in Unity using Playmaker and C#, it is a PlayStation 1-style horror game reminiscent of Resident Evil. The player navigates through a mansion infested with zombie-like enemies. The player will find a flashlight that will need batteries. The battery meter on the HUD slowly depletes, going from green to orange to red, and the light will progressively get dimmer. They will find a pistol that will need ammunition. The regular enemies have damage-sensitive hitboxes: one shot to the head, 3-5 to the body, and more to the limbs to stop them. There are riddles and puzzles throughout the game that the player will have to solve to progress. The final boss will teleport and despawn randomly around the player in a final battle in the maze of furniture in the attic of the mansion.

Unreal Obstacle

Unreal Obstacle is a third person platformer written entirely in C++ in the Unreal Engine. Climb to the top of the level, navigating moving obstacles such as spinning platforms, rising and falling platofrms and pushing blocks. There is a checkpoint system in place. When the player gets past an obstacle section but falls off the world, they will be respawned at a point before the next obstacle.

Music Room Wreckage

Music Room Wreckage is a physics based destruction game. I was made in the Unreal Engine using C++. The player starts in a music room where every object has physics and gravity attached. The plaer can navigate the room and shoot balls. The player can shoot 30 balls to destroy the room. When all the balls are used, the room will reset.
