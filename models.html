Dragon

My rigged dragon model, made in Blender using techniques such as retopology. The model features a high-resolution version hidden behind a game-ready version for optimal performance, with a much smaller tri and vert count. Using sculpting brushes, texture baking, and texture painting, the model is finished to a high standard, then rigged, animated, and posed.
